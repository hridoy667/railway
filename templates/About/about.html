About Us

Welcome to SymptoCare, your web-based partner in personalized health insights and symptom analysis. Our platform offers a simple yet effective approach to managing personal health, empowering users with insights and guidance tailored to their unique health profiles.

Our Mission

At SymptoCare, we believe that everyone deserves accessible and personalized health insights. Our mission is to provide an intuitive, data-driven platform that promotes proactive health management through easy-to-understand recommendations and resources for daily well-being.

What We Offer

Our system leverages inputs such as symptoms, age, height, and weight to deliver personalized health guidance. With a hidden BMI-based approach, users receive health suggestions that align with their needs without any overwhelming data exposure. Key features include:

  • Risk assesment Tool: Understand and monitor your symptoms with insightful suggestions.
  • Personalized Tips: Receive dietary, exercise, and lifestyle tips aligned to your health profile.
  • Time-based Suggestions:We give time based suggestions for various activity and diet.
  • Mindfullness Helps keeping you motivated, makes you practice mindfullness.
