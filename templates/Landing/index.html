SymptoCare offers a user-friendly and efficient disease diagnosis system. By analyzing symptoms provided by the user along with hidden BMI data, it delivers personalized health insights. This feature helps users understand potential health risks and suggests preventive measures and lifestyle adjustments to maintain well-being. The diagnosis process is simple yet powerful, aimed at promoting early detection of chronic conditions and offering guidance on managing health more effectively.


SymptoCare provides personalized food and diet recommendations based on a user's health profile and BMI analysis. By considering potential health risks, dietary restrictions, and the user's body structure, this feature offers tailored meal plans that promote a balanced and nutritious lifestyle. These suggestions aim to support users in achieving optimal health, preventing diet-related illnesses, and improving overall quality of life.

SymptoCareassists users in maintaining a balanced lifestyle through its integrated Work-Life Balance feature.This feature helps users plan their daily tasks by incorporating notifications for Deep Work Sessions and Nap Sessions, ensuring they stay productive and rejuvenated throughout the day.Users can focus on their work schedules, set priorities, and manage time more efficiently with guided deep work notifications, while restorative nap reminders boost cognitive function and energy levels. This feature ensures that users maintain a healthy balance between their personal and professional life, which is critical for overall health and well-being.
