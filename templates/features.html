Features

Risk Assesment Tool

Our Symptom Checker feature empowers users to input symptoms and receive potential diagnoses, using BMI to tailor suggestions. This feature guides proactive health management, allowing users to catch warning signs early and consult with professionals promptly.

Personalized Health Dashboard

Stay on top of your health goals with our personalized dashboard. This central hub displays your symptom history, risk alerts, and tailored health tips, making it easy to track your wellness journey and make informed decisions for better health outcomes.

Work-Life Balance Tools

Our system supports your work-life balance with features like time based deep work suggestions,nap or relaxing suggestion and daily wellness tips for boosting productivity. These tools are designed to keep you motivated and maintain a harmonious balance between work and health.

Health Tips and Recommendations

Receive curated health tips and advice based on your profile and lifestyle. From diet suggestions to stress management tips, our system supports every aspect of your well-being, providing actionable steps to improve your quality of life.
